## 🎯 Overview

The Tattoo Pain Map & Guide is an interactive resource designed to help clients understand and prepare for the varying levels of discomfort associated with different tattoo placements. It provides a visual heatmap and expert insights to manage expectations and ensure a better tattooing experience.

**Key Use Cases:**

- Researching pain levels for specific placements
- Planning session lengths based on area sensitivity
- Preparing for first-time tattoos

**Target Audience:**

- Tattoo clients
- Artists providing consultation

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Interactive Pain Map** - Visual guide to sensitivity across the body.
- ✅ **Detailed Area Analysis** - Deep dives into specific body parts.
- ✅ **Preparation Tips** - Expert advice on how to minimize discomfort.
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- ✅ **Free & Open Source** - MIT licensed.
